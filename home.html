FAVORITES WITH TABLE (called bookmarks in most browsers) are a great way to save and organize websites so you can revisit them. 1.With the desired website open in your browser, select the Favorites button, then click Add to favorites. You can also press Ctrl+D on your keyboard. 2.A dialog box will appear. To choose a folder for your favorite, click the drop-down menu in the Create in: box, then select a folder. 3. Click Add to save the website as a favorite.

USER'S INFO is information transferred across the functional interface between a source user and a telecommunications system for delivery to a destination user. In telecommunications systems, user information includes user overhead information.

FAMILY is a group of people related either by consanguinity (by recognized birth) or affinity (by marriage or other relationship). The purpose of the family is to maintain the well-being of its members and of society. Ideally, families offer predictability, structure, and safety as members mature and learn to participate in the community.[1] In most human societies, the family is the primary locus of attachment, nurturance, and socialization.
